Special Leave Petition Nos. 823 24 of 1990 . The petitioners were the owners of certain lands which were acquired by the respondents under the provisions of Sections 17 and 19 of the Bangalore Development Act, 1976 . Section 11 A of the Land Acquisition Act, which section was included in the said Act in 1984, provides that the Collector must make his award within two years from the date of the publication of the declaration .
