This is an appeal by special leave against the judgment and order of the High Court of Bombay reversing the judgment of the Chief Presidency Magistrate, Bombay, in Case No. 176/S of 1953. The appellants are the Andheri Marol Kurla Bus Service who was accused No. 1 and its Manager H. M. Khan (now appellant No. 2) The question for decision is whether the conciliation proceedings could be said to be pending when Louis Pereira was dismissed. If the answer is in the affirmative the appellants have been properly convicted and if not the conviction must be set aside.
